FAQ

How much will my project cost?

Home renovations can be expensive but since you plan to do most the work yourself it will cutdown on costs. Kitchen's tend to take the biggest chunk of the budget, followed by bathrooms. it also depends then on your landsaping wants.


What are the latest trends in home renovation?

I always tell my clients not to follow trends as they come in and out so quickly and it is not cheap to have to redo your home in five years time.


Should I hire a contractor or do it myself?

It depends on several factors such as your skills, experience, time availability, and the complexity of the project. For major renovations or projects requiring specialised skills, it's generally recommended to hire a licensed and experienced contractor. However, smaller projects or ones within your expertise can be done yourself, provided you have the necessary knowledge and tools.


How long does a typical home renovation project take?

The duration of a home renovation project depends on the scope and complexity of the work. Small projects such as painting or flooring replacement can be completed in a few days to weeks. Larger renovations like kitchen remodels or whole-house renovations can take several weeks to months. It's essential to discuss the timeline with your contractor and account for any potential delays.
